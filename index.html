FAQ

Common Questions

How do I use my key in-game? ›
Type /secretaappopen in Roblox chat, or press F2 in-game. A login panel appears. Enter your username and key, click Authenticate, and wait 7 seconds.
Why doesn't my key work? ›
If you generated a key through this site, it's sent automatically to the game server. It may take up to 30 seconds to activate. If it still doesn't work after a minute, contact Kshusha319.
Can I share my key? ›
No. Your key is personal. Sharing it violates the rules and will get it revoked. Your friend should generate their own.
Can I type a random key to get in? ›
No. The game server validates every login against a live database. If the key wasn't generated here, access is denied every time.
How do I get Admin or higher? ›
Admin and above are invitation-only. Contact Kshusha319 directly if you believe you qualify. They are not available through this portal.
Is my key permanent? ›
Keys are valid until they are revoked by an admin. They persist across sessions — you only need to enter it once per game session.
